## News details

### paypal-invoicing-2026-06-01-paypal-refreshes-invoicing-api-docs-for-create-send-manage-reminder-and-refund-flows

PayPal's [Invoicing API documentation](https://developer.paypal.com/docs/invoicing/) now presents create, send, manage, reminder, and refund flows as a developer-facing invoicing surface.

For buyers, this reframes PayPal Invoicing as more than a manual invoice dashboard. A software company or platform can evaluate PayPal as an embeddable invoicing layer, especially when the workflow needs to live inside an existing product experience rather than a separate finance tool.

- Buyer signal: Relevant for teams that need API-backed invoicing but do not need a full subscription billing suite.
- Follow-up: Verify API coverage for invoice lifecycle events, reminders, partial payments, refunds, webhooks, reconciliation, and accounting exports.

### paypal-invoicing-2026-02-25-rainforest-launches-embedded-paypal-integration-for-vertical-software-platforms

Rainforest launched an embedded PayPal integration for vertical software platforms, according to PayPal's [corporate newsroom release](https://newsroom.paypal-corp.com/2026-02-25-Rainforest-Launches-Embedded-PayPal-Integration-for-Vertical-Software-Platforms).

The buyer angle is distribution. Vertical SaaS companies often need to let merchants accept familiar payment methods without becoming payment infrastructure companies themselves. PayPal, Venmo, Pay Later, cards, Apple Pay, and Pay by Bank support can matter when the buyer is building merchant-facing workflows.

- Buyer signal: PayPal remains relevant as an embedded payment option for platforms, not only as a consumer checkout brand.
- Follow-up: Compare onboarding, merchant underwriting, settlement, fees, dispute handling, and how embedded PayPal payments connect to invoicing or subscription flows.

### paypal-invoicing-2025-04-29-paypal-announces-developer-tools-for-agentic-commerce

PayPal announced developer tools for agentic commerce in a [PayPal-issued PR Newswire release](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/paypal-brings-together-developers-ai-leaders-to-power-agentic-commerce-at-dev-days-302441418.html).

The near-term subscription billing tie is exploratory but important. As AI agents start initiating purchases, checking order status, or creating payment links, finance and product teams will need payment tools that are explicit about authorization, user intent, and transaction control.

- Buyer signal: Relevant for teams thinking about how AI agents may interact with invoicing and payment workflows.
- Follow-up: Ask what is production-ready, what safeguards exist for agent-initiated actions, and how PayPal distinguishes discovery, authorization, and payment execution.
