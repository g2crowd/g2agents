### Live feature refresh - reviewed 2026-07-01

Maxio is positioned as a subscription billing and financial operations platform for B2B SaaS and AI companies. The strongest public evidence comes from Maxio's [pricing table](https://www.maxio.com/pricing), product pages, and documentation.

#### Core capabilities with public evidence

| Capability | Public evidence level | Notes for buyers |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Subscription billing and recurring billing | Public vendor documentation | Pricing page lists automated invoicing and billing, recurring billing, fixed billing, usage-based billing, and subscription management. |
| Product catalog and subscription lifecycle management | Public vendor documentation | Maxio documents product catalog, contract terms, self-service signup pages, free trials, customer portal, and renewal management. |
| Usage-based / metered billing | Public vendor documentation | Maxio lists usage-based billing in all plans and documents prepaid subscriptions for metered or event-based components. |
| Collections and dunning | Public vendor documentation | Pricing page lists collections and dunning; docs describe dunning schedules for card, invoice/manual remittance, ACH/eCheck, and direct debit scenarios. |
| Invoicing, manual collection, and consolidated invoices | Public vendor documentation | Docs describe automatic billing, invoice billing, payment terms, and consolidated invoicing for multiple subscriptions. |
| Revenue recognition and deferred revenue reporting | Public vendor documentation | Pricing table lists scheduled revenue, recognized revenue, deferred revenue, performance obligations, SSPs, carveouts, multiple revenue books, and reporting outputs. |
| A/R and finance reporting | Public vendor documentation | Pricing table lists A/R aging, roll forwards, contract details, payment summary, DSO, SaaS metric reporting, cohorts, renewal rate, and subscription momentum reports. |
| Customer portal / self-service | Public vendor documentation | Billing Portal docs state customers can manage subscriptions, payment methods, invoices, and account details, subject to configuration. |
| Payment processing / gateways | Public vendor documentation | Pricing page lists 20+ payment gateways and Maxio Payments as a payments option. |

#### Edition-dependent or quote-dependent capabilities

The following are publicly listed, but buyers should confirm plan inclusion and pricing because Maxio presents them as advanced features, optional modules, or add-ons: Advanced Revenue Management, Advanced A/R Management, Expense Amortization, Milestone-based Projects, Multi-entity, Event-based Billing, EU/EMEA Hosting, Enterprise SSO, Maxio Metrics, Multi-Site Reporting, Multi-Currency, API, and Premium Support.

#### Unverified or requires vendor confirmation

- Exact feature differences between "standard" and "advanced" financial reporting, A/R management, and revenue recognition.
- Limits for invoices, subscriptions, customers, API calls, webhooks, entities, currencies, and sites.
- Whether CPQ capabilities are included in the quoted Maxio subscription or sold separately.
- Support for specific accounting policies or ASC 606 / IFRS 15 workflows should be validated with sample contracts and revenue schedules, not assumed from category fit.
