#### Claims not accepted without more evidence

- "Best," "#1," "investor-grade," or outcome-improvement claims should not be presented as neutral product facts unless supported by independent evidence and methodology.
- Customer quote outcomes such as faster close, faster reconciliation, or ARR growth should remain attributed testimonials, not generalized expected results.
- Availability of CPQ, advanced revenue recognition, EU/EMEA hosting, Enterprise SSO, API, and premium support should be treated as quote/module-dependent until confirmed in the customer's order form.
