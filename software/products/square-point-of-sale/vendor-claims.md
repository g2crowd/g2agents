### Vendor-attested claim candidates - researched 2026-07-01

The following are **candidate vendor claims** found in Square/Block public materials. They should not be treated as independent G2-verified truth unless separately validated.

| Candidate claim | Vendor-attested evidence | Buyer validation question |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Square POS has a Free plan with no monthly subscription cost. | Square's public pricing says Square Free has no monthly subscription cost and sellers pay processing fees when taking payments. | Does this apply to your country, account type, and required features? |
| Square Plus and Premium are month-to-month and include a 30-day trial. | Square pricing FAQ says Plus and Premium offer a free 30-day trial and sellers can cancel or switch plans anytime. | Are any add-ons, hardware financing, marketplace apps, or custom terms separately contracted? |
| High-volume sellers may qualify for custom pricing. | Square says sellers processing over $250,000/year can contact Square about custom processing and pricing. | What volume, ticket size, risk profile, and contract term are required for the quote? |
| Square supports card-present, online, invoice, ACH, keyed/card-on-file, Afterpay, cash/check, and bitcoin payment methods. | Square's public fee table and POS feature matrix list these payment types and rates/rules. | Which methods are enabled for your region, MCC, hardware, and risk profile? |
| Square provides integrated POS, online selling, invoicing, booking, item library, inventory, reporting, customer directory, loyalty/marketing, and staff tools. | Square's POS feature matrix lists these modules and capabilities. | Which are included in Free versus Plus/Premium/custom plans, and which require add-ons? |
| Square App Marketplace supports third-party integrations. | Square support says sellers can connect partner apps from App Marketplace and that partner features/services/subscriptions are managed directly by app partners. | Who owns support, costs, uptime, data mapping, and sync-error resolution? |
| Square offers developer APIs and SDKs. | Square's developer site references more than 20 APIs, 100+ endpoints, server-side SDKs, and mobile/frontend payment SDKs. | What rate limits, sandbox coverage, webhook guarantees, and API deprecations apply? |
| Square's card-processing systems meet PCI DSS Level 1 and Square is ISO 27001 certified. | Square security pages list PCI DSS Level 1 and ISO 27001 credentials, and refer buyers to Trust Center for PCI/SOC documentation. | Request current certificate/report scope, audit period, and product/service coverage. |

#### Claims not established from public materials reviewed

- Public pages reviewed did not establish a complete data residency commitment for Square Point of Sale.
- Public pages reviewed did not disclose full SOC report contents, penetration-test reports, or detailed uptime SLA terms.
- Public pages reviewed did not prove that every App Marketplace integration is Square-built, bidirectional, or covered by Square support.
