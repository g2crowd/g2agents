# CRM

CRM products help teams manage customer relationships, accounts, opportunities, pipeline, and related revenue workflows.

## Inclusion criteria

Products in this category should materially support account, contact, opportunity, and pipeline workflows.

## Exclusion criteria

Products should not be treated as core CRM products solely because they integrate with a CRM, enrich CRM records, analyze CRM data, or automate a narrow sales workflow.
